Our Story

Born from a Passion
for Ethiopian Heritage

Geda Restaurant was founded by an Ethiopian family who carried their grandmother's recipes across borders to share with the world. Our kitchen follows traditions passed down for generations — no shortcuts, no compromises.

Whether you're discovering Ethiopian cuisine for the first time or craving the flavors of home, we prepare every dish with the same care and warmth we'd show our own family.
